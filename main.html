The Cal Lodge is a historic building built by U.C. Student volunteers from the Department of Forestry in the late 1930s. The Lodge is owned by the Associated Students of the University of California, a non-profit organization which also functions as the student government at the University of California at Berkeley.


The Lodge features Old Tahoe style architecture and is surrounded by a beautiful forest setting. The Lodge is open all season. During the summer it provides an excellent retreat for activities such as hiking, sailing, biking and rafting. Cal Lodge is now open and everyone is invited to come on up and enjoy affordable rates at our beautiful Lake Tahoe location. Student discounts are available and groups are encouraged to come up together for the best rates. The Lodge is open to Cal students, faculty, staff and the general public. Enjoy skiing, snowboarding, hiking, fishing, biking and much more -- the opportunities for fun are endless!
